# Tableau Marketing Analytics Dashboards

## Overview
This repository showcases interactive Tableau dashboards developed as part of
marketing analytics, customer insights, and business analytics projects.
The dashboards focus on data visualization, storytelling, and
decision-support for business stakeholders.

---

## Dashboard 1: Customer Service Satisfaction Drivers (Capstone)

**Objective:**
Analyze key drivers influencing customer satisfaction (CSAT) in an
e-commerce customer service environment.

**Key Analyses:**
- CSAT trends by service and agent attributes
- Issue-level satisfaction patterns
- Identification of high- and low-performing service segments

**Business Value:**
Supports customer experience improvement and service optimization strategies.

---

## Dashboard 2: Coffee Chain Sales & Profit Analysis

**Objective:**
Evaluate sales and profit performance across product types, market sizes,
states, and regions to support business decision-making.

**Key Analyses:**
- Sales comparison by product type and market size
- Profitability analysis by region and product
- Identification of loss-making products and markets
- Top-performing states for coffee sales

**Key Insights:**
- Major markets generate significantly higher sales across most product types
- Coffee has the highest profit; Espresso has the highest sales
- California leads coffee sales
- Certain products show negative profits in specific regions

**Business Value:**
Helps product managers and executives identify growth opportunities and
areas requiring cost or strategy optimization.
